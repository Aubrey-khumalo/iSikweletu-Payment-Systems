The implications behind iSikweletu

Isikweletu Simplified Secure Payments is built for simplicity and localization. The registered user can benefit from the features of electrical bill or purchace, airtime and reciept or payment of cash provided by the MoMo API. The API is interfaced and the website is used for the front end user to be simply acquainted with the features provided by this API. Using iSikweletu a registered user can prompt or remind another user to pay their fees or the debt that they are owed. The prompt recieve can dicide to decline or accept the request to pay sent by the debt collector.

A database is used at the back end to store the registered users and transaction history. This databse is simple and has no more than two tables. Isikweletu is driven to be a potential useful tool for many and any kind of community. Landlord and there tenants can use this website or payment method to settle their periodic payments. A debit order can also be placed within the functionalities of the website. The ease of use and efficiency allows.

Our Story

During the corona pandemic people where restricted to public excess and lockdrown regulations where put in place as to prevent spread of corona virus. During these prevention methods many people that largely relied on contact exchage of money had to postpone there endevours. At the University of Zululand there are campus residences that are owned by community locals which students rent. During the national lockdown universities and schools where ordered by the president to close. This closure resulted in students returning home and not being able to pay their landlord the money due to her/him for the room which currently contains their belongings.

People that were separated during the lockdown without having to be paid their money they can use iSikweletu and the number of the person that owes them to make a request for them to pay them back their money. This way you can be reminded to setle your debts if you had forgotten or just so you can concider prioritizing paying back the money you owe.
